Jessica Jones
Season 1 (2015)

Former superhero Jessica Jones works as a private investigator in New York, haunted by past trauma. When her abuser Kilgrave, a man with mind-control powers, resurfaces, she’s forced to face him. Across the season, Jessica battles PTSD, protects others from Kilgrave, and ultimately kills him, reclaiming control over her life.

Jessica Jones
Season 2 (2018)

Jessica continues working as a private investigator while coping with the aftermath of killing Kilgrave. She begins investigating the mysterious organization IGH, responsible for the experiments that gave her powers. During her search, Jessica discovers that her mother, Alisa Jones, survived the car crash that killed her family.

Jessica Jones
Season 3 (2019)

In the final season, Jessica faces a new threat: Gregory Sallinger, a brilliant but sadistic serial killer who despises powered people. As Jessica hunts him, she clashes with her former best friend Trish Walker, who has now gained powers and taken on the role of vigilante “Hellcat.” The season explores their fractured relationship as Trish’s desire for justice spirals into vengeance.
